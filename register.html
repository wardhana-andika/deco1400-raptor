Registration Information

For 2015 Exhibit entry & public camping forms PLEASE EMAIL US.

If you wish to bring you vintage car or motorcycle for the day, we now have a turn up on the day of your choice. It is not necessary to book, just turn up!

Please do not send in a form for 'Turn up on the day' entries. Vintage vehicles must be within the historic vehicle road tax category. Later dated vehicles must be of special interest, for example Harley Davidson, Rolls Royce of Luxury type.

Entries that are not attending for the full 3 days are invited to attend. This is done on a 'turn up on the day' basis. You may attend with your exhibit on the day of your choice. At previous shows we have allowed vehicles of interest on to the site. A 50% discount will apply per occupant. Prior booking is not necessary. Your entry will be able to take full part in the show including arena activities. Vehicle admission is at the discretion of the gate staff. In the interest of safety and the betterment of the event, we do ask that you remain on the show-ground until the daily end of the show: SAT, SUN 5:00PM. MON 4:30PM.
