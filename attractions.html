Attractions

Fighter Jet Flyover

Our show will open with a series of flyovers from a squadron of fighter jets. Make sure you arrive on time to witness the awe-inspiring acrobatics performed by the pilots. From the AFX1958 to the FXK2592 fighter jet, you do NOT want to miss their majestic flight!

Bike Show

There will be hundreds of bikes participating in our bike show. Bikes of various types and origins will be open for viewing. There will also be a show of bike tricks performed by some of the participants.

Steam Engine Exhibition

Our main attraction will feature steam-based vehicles of various sizes. Special imports from Holland, Greece, France, Russia, you name it, we have it! Whether you were interested in steam vehicles beforehand or not, we guarantee you will be enlightened!

Antique Car Exhibition

For car enthusiasts, this attraction will feature tens of cars for visitors to view. This exhibition will feature many limited edition vehicles and refurbished old-school cars.

Local Delicacies

Our show will also have food stalls featuring local delicacies. Visitors of our previous shows will remember the fantastic 'special beef' and soup, along with our famous 'rush hour' food stall sales! You do not want to miss out!

Souvenir and Textile Stands

For someone who wants something to remember this show by, our show will have many market stalls selling unique souvenirs and textiles.
