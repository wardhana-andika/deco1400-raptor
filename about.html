Our Show

This family run show is now in it's 29th year and is one of the last remaining events of its kind. It is organised and run in the same way as it was done years ago.
NEW EXHIBITORS, TRADERS & PUBLIC CAMPERS WELCOME.

With all the vehicles and machinery, children's animal area, craft marquee, food hall, huge auto-jumble, wild west shot out shows, acres of market, machinery displays & on sale. Arena displays, many live demonstrations, mini beer festival with agricultural music from the new forest Yokels. For list, see our leaflet on ... .An excellent day out for all the family!
